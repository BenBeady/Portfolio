How I Work

I work fully remotely and am comfortable with asynchronous communication. I use clear documentation and consistent updates so clients always know where a project stands.

Before any work begins, I make sure we agree on what is being built, how long it will take, and what it will cost. I would rather have a direct conversation about scope upfront than a difficult one later.

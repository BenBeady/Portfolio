A Few Things to Know Upfront

I Am Selective About Projects

I take on a small number of projects at a time so each one gets real attention. If your project is not a fit for my current availability or skill set, I will tell you honestly. I would rather do that than overpromise.

Scope and Cost Are Agreed Upfront

Before any work begins, we agree on exactly what is being built, what it will cost, and when it will be done. No vague retainers, no scope creep surprises.

You Will Own Everything

When the project is complete, you own the code, the domain, the hosting, all of it. I do not hold your site hostage or lock you into recurring fees you did not ask for.

A few things to know upfront

I'm selective about projects

I take on a small number of projects at a time so each one gets real attention. If your project isn't a fit for my current availability or skill set, I'll tell you honestly — I'd rather do that than overpromise.

Scope and cost are agreed upfront

Before any work begins, we agree on exactly what's being built, what it will cost, and when it will be done. No vague retainers, no scope creep surprises.

You'll own everything

When the project is complete, you own the code, the domain, the hosting — all of it. I don't hold your site hostage or lock you into recurring fees you didn't ask for.
